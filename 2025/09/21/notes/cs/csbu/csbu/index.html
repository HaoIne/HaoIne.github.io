Now it’s 2024-9-21, I’ve been reading the book Computer Science from the Bottom Up

I’m reading this book with destinations below:

  1. To learn the base of computer architecture and composing, operating system and assembly.
  2. To improve my English reading ability.

And of course, as the first English book I’m reading, this task could be tricky, and the experience may be painful. I would be struggling with those tricky words, complex terms and long sentences.

So, as the start, completely comprehending the knowledge and instances, principles, etc. is not ranked first. I know I’ll go better.

And now I’m talking about my plan with this book: I will work over this book for just one month, or at most one week more. Because I know that the speed of reading also matters, and need to be developed.

From today on, I’ll take notes every day about what I’ve learned at that day, and summarize what I’ve learn in that week.
